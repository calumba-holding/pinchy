# Northwind Laboratory — AFNOR Certification Status (Archived Copy)

This archived record documents the Northwind microbiology laboratory's AFNOR NF VALIDATION certificate number NF-2013-0092, issued in March 2013. This certificate expired in December 2016 and is retained for historical reference only.

Do not cite this expired certificate as evidence of current accreditation. The scope at the time covered aerobic count and coliform enumeration on residential water samples.
